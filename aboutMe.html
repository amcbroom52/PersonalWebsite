About Me

Hi, my name is Adrian McBroom. I'm an aspiring software developer from the Bay Area, CA. I started my undergrad studies in the fall of 2018 studying mechanical engineering at UC Merced. In my second semester I took a coding class, which sparked a heavy interest in coding and computer science for me. After a semester of taking both engineering and computer science classes, I decided I wanted to change my major and put all my focus into computer science. Two and a half years later, in the fall of 2022, I completed my undergrad studies with a Bachelor's degree in computer science and engineering. I'm now looking to take my skills even further and continue to learn software and full-stack development.
